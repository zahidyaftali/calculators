What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is a simple tool that computes your exact age from your date of birth to today's date. Unlike a simple subtraction of years, a precise age calculator accounts for leap years, varying month lengths, and whether your birthday has occurred yet in the current year.

Our Age Calculator goes beyond just years — it tells you your age in years, months, and days with complete accuracy. It also provides additional statistics such as your total age in days, weeks, and total months, as well as the number of days remaining until your next birthday.

How the Age Calculator Works

The calculation follows a precise three-step process:

Step 1: Years = Current Year − Birth Year
Step 2: Adjust for months (if current month < birth month, subtract 1 year)
Step 3: Adjust for days (if current day < birth day, subtract 1 month, add days in previous month)

For example, if you were born on March 15, 1990, and today is April 25, 2025: You are 35 years, 1 month, and 10 days old. Total days = 12,824.

Why Calculate Your Exact Age?

  • Legal purposes: Age verification for contracts, retirement benefits, and eligibility checks.
  • Medical use: Doctors calculate precise ages for pediatric dosing, developmental milestones, and screening recommendations.
  • Fun statistics: Discover how many days or weeks you've been alive.
  • Countdown to birthday: Know exactly how many days until your next celebration.
  • HR and admin: Companies use age calculations for employee benefits, retirement planning, and compliance.

Leap Year Handling

Leap years occur every 4 years (with some exceptions for century years). A leap year has 366 days instead of 365. Our calculator automatically accounts for leap years when computing total days, ensuring maximum accuracy. If you were born on February 29 (a leap day), the calculator correctly handles the next birthday as either February 28 or March 1 depending on the year.

Fun Age Facts

  • An average person's heart beats about 100,000 times per day — multiply that by your total days!
  • By age 70, the average person will have slept for approximately 24 years.
  • The oldest verified human ever lived was Jeanne Calment of France, who died at 122 years and 164 days old.
  • In some cultures, the traditional way of counting age counts the year of birth as "year one," making everyone born a year older.
